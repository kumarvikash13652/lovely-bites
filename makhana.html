The Authentic Geographic Origin: Mithila, Bihar

Our Makhana isn't just a snack—it is part of an ancient ecological lineage. Over 80% of the world's premium aquatic foxnut production comes directly from the unique perennial water bodies and wetlands of the Mithila region in Bihar, India.

The special heavy clay-loam waterbeds of Mithila naturally feed the crops with dense biological micro-nutrients. Local farmers have preserved these non-destructive harvesting methods for generations, manually handling the seeds from deep underwater without any automated industrial chemicals. Choosing this product directly preserves these natural setups and guarantees pure quality.

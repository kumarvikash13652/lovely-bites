Traditional Delicacies

Easy culinary ideas made using native pure ingredients

Roasted Spicy Makhana

Lightly toss Mithila Makhana in ghee, black salt, and pepper for an ultra-healthy immunity booster alternative to popcorn.

Refreshing Sattu Sharbat

Mix 3 spoons of clean Chana Sattu in chilled water, add lemon juice, roasted cumin powder, and mint for an active energetic day drink.

Bihari Katarni Dahi Chura

Wash the fragrant Katarni Poha under clean water and instantly layer with fresh thick curd and jaggery powder for an authentic local morning breakfast.
